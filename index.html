Betroot salad

Russianfood
Ingredient Amount
Beetroot salad 1 kg
Potatoe 4 pcs
Beetroot 1 pcs
Carrot 4 pcs
Pickles 3 pcs
Onion 1 pcs
Salad dressing 0,5-1 glass
Salt to taste
  1. Step 1: Finely chop the greens
  2. Step 2: Prepare the dressing for the vinaigrette. To do this, mix sugar, salt, pepper, and vinegar. Add chopped greens and vegetable oil. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
  3. Step 3: Bring water to a boil in separate pots. In boiling salted water, add potatoes and carrots, and cook for approximately 25-30 minutes.
  4. Step 4: In another pot, add a little vinegar. Place beets in the boiling acidified water and cook for 30-40 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Pour cold water over the cooked vegetables, cool them, and peel. Dice the potatoes, beets, carrots, cabbage, and pickles.
  6. Step 6: Combine all the vegetables, add cabbage and beans, pour the vinaigrette dressing, and mix well.
